# DSP-DIWBIKES Wiki

Welcome to the **DSP-DIWBIKES (Do It Now Bikes)** data model and business context wiki.

DSP-DIWBIKES is a fictitious global bicycle manufacturing and retail company designed to demonstrate:
- Relational data modeling
- Business-to-data alignment
- Enterprise domain separation
- Analytics-ready schemas

This wiki documents the **business overview**, **data domains**, and **entity relationships** that underpin the DSP-DIWBIKES database.
